[136:1] Give ye thanks to Jehovah, For good, for to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:2] Give ye thanks to the God of gods, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:3] Give ye thanks to the Lord of lords, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:4] To Him doing great wonders by Himself alone, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:5] To Him making the heavens by understanding, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:6] To Him spreading the earth over the waters, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:7] To Him making great lights, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:8] The sun to rule by day, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:9] The moon and stars to rule by night, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:10] To Him smiting Egypt in their first-born, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:11] And bringing forth Israel from their midst, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:12] By a strong hand, and a stretched-out-arm, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:13] To Him cutting the sea of Suph into parts, For to the age `is' His kindness,
[136:14] And caused Israel to pass through its midst, For to the age `is' His kindness,
[136:15] And shook out Pharaoh and his force in the sea of Suph, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:16] To Him leading His people in a wilderness, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:17] To Him smiting great kings, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:18] Yea, He doth slay honourable kings, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:19] Even Sihon king of the Amorite, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:20] And Og king of Bashan, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:21] And He gave their land for inheritance, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:22] An inheritance to Israel His servant, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:23] Who in our lowliness hath remembered us, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:24] And He delivereth us from our adversaries, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:25] Giving food to all flesh, For to the age `is' His kindness.
[136:26] Give ye thanks to the God of the heavens, For to the age `is' His kindness!
